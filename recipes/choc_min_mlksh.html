Chocolate-Mint Milkshake

Description

Is it cold or warm? Matters not! This delicious milkshake will make kids and adults alike wonder: how I've been living without this!?

Ingredients

  • 2 ⅔ cups white sugar
  • ¼ cup milk
  • ¼ cup chocolate syrup
  • 1 drop peppermint extract

Steps

  1. Combine ice cream, milk, chocolate syrup, and peppermint extract in a blender; blend until smooth.
  2. Pour into 2 glasses and serve.
